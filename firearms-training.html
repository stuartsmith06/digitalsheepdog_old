Firearms Training

A week doesn't go by when I hear my wife, one of my children, or a friend tell me that "so and so wants to buy a gun."  In today's world, I think many people are starting to take their personal safety and security seriously.  This is a good thing!  Our right to self-defense is God-given, and I believe every law abiding, responsible adult should avail themselves of their Second Amendment rights.

Regardless if you own, or want to purchase, a handgun, shotgun, or rifle, you should strongly consider getting training in the safe use of firearms.  As a U.S. Army veteran and Certified NRA Firearms instructor, I would be honored to provide you, your spouse, or even your entire family quality, effective, and personal firearms safety training.

My courses are flexible, affordable, and fun!  Whether you already own a firearm, or you're looking for help in deciding which gun to purchase, I can help.  If you live in the Atlanta area, let's talk!
